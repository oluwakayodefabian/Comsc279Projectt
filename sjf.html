Shortest Job First

Shortest job first (SJF) is a scheduling algorithm that selects the waiting process with the smallest execution time to be executed next

Sample Analogy: Imagine a laundromat where the attendant processes clothes. If someone comes with only a single shirt, their job might be prioritized over someone with a large pile of laundry.
