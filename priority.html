Priority-Scheduling

Priority-Scheduling is a pre-emptive method of CPU scheduling algorithm that works based on the priority of a process. In this type Cpu scheduling algorithm, processes are assigned priorities, and the highest - priority process executes first. An advantage that priority-Scheduling has is that it handles critical tasks effectively and a disavantage is that low-priority process may face starvation.

Sample Analogy: In the hospital, a doctor stops attending to a patient with a minor injury to save someone having a heart attack or was rushed to the emergency room.
